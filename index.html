about me

close
I wanted to be a hair designer since the age of ten & accomplished that goal over 10 years ago. I am passionate about continuing in advanced hair education in order to grow my skills & deliver the best product to my clientele.
My first love has always been haircolor. I only use Aveda haircolor since it is so eco-friendly, contains nourishing organic oils, is superior in gray coverage, & is completely customizable making it possible to deliver the perfect haircolor shade for you.
Hair shaping, formal hairstyles, & hair straighteners are also my specialties. I customize all my haircuts to my guests face shape, lifestyle, & hair texture. I texturize my haircuts when they are dry so I can best see how the hair will fall. I would love to make your special days even more special by creating a hair design that demands attention. I have done many formal hairstyles for fashion shows & love the creative challenge they present. Although keratin & Japanese straighteners are getting a lot of negative publicity, I have kept up on research, know which ones are safe & deliver an excellent result, & have one in my hair almost at any given time.
My goal is for you to leave feeling beautiful and with the knowledge of how to achieve it yourself!
Sincerely,
Magan
